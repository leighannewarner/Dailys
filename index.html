About Me & Dailys

  • I am 22 and just graduated with a degree in computer science.

  • Dailys is open to anyone, but I designed this tool for people with processing disorders like ADD. Some people with processing disorders have difficulty with traditional to do lists because there is too much information available at once. By showing only one item at a time, I hoped to mitigate this issue.

  • This tool was designed with a lot of input from my boyfriend.

  • Dailys was programmed with javaScript and localstorage. This means your data is stored on your device, not a server. It also means it should still work for people with a spotty internet connection. Once the page loads, you should have no issues with Dailys regardless of your internet.
